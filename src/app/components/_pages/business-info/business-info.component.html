1. Main Form of Contact

~ Who will be your main form of contact? Only one person with authority is needed to keep in touch with Chaos & Art for ease of development. In some circumstances two people with authority is okay, but one is preferred. This person will be accountable for responding to my queries, processing my invoices and reviewing my work. This person also has the authority to communicate, make changes, and ultimately guid the project to its final draft.

2. Fees and Payments

~ Fees and Payments depend on the type of project. A major project from beginning to end will run more expensive, while touch ups or simple changes on an existing project are cheaper.


~ After brainstorming and determining what the client wants, we'll settle on a price. In some cases Chaos & Art may charge an hourly rate instead of a fixed price. If the price is fixed, we take a 35% security deposit of the total price, and will charge the remaining on completetion. For hourly rates, we request payment per every 24 hours of work until completetion of the project.


~ Why a Security Deposit? A deposit will let you secure a spot in our schedule. If a client hasn't booked their position in advance, we cannot guarantee we'll work on your project when you need us to. Someone who has paid the security deposit will have our time allocated to them.


2.1 Late Payment Penalties

~ There are Late Payment Penalties set in place. Penalties will differ upon the project and will be explicitly stated within each contract. One example of a penalty would be a 5% additional charge for every 7 days without payment.


~ Chaos & Art understands finances can be tight at times, and under the circumstance may alter a late penalty in favor of the client. Refer to your personal contract for more details.

3. Cancellation and Kill Fee

~ A cancellation fee will be dependent on 2 different phases within the project.

1: The Beginning. If the project is in the beginning stages and we have determined that much of our time hasn't been used on your project we'll offer a full refund including the initial deposit. (Usually within the first few hours on small projects, and days on bigger projects.)


2: The Middle and near the End. If the project is cancelled within the middle or near the end of development, we keep the inital security deposit, and will charge a kill fee. A kill fee is compensation for the time that we spent working on your project, and rates depend on how much time was spent working on said project. Projects near the end of their completion will be 75% to 90% of remaining project price while projects midway through production will be 50% of remaing project price.

4. Changes and Revisions

~ Once we have finished the project the client will have 24 hours to make note of any changes they'd like to make. After the 24 hours, Chaos & Art will make changes accordingly, all included in the original price point and submit the final product to the client. For additional and future changes, an hourly rate will be applied.


4.1 Additional Options

~ Clients who had Websites built for them have a couple options once their project has been completed. The first option would be to take the completed content and do what they will with it. For the second option, Chaos & Art can take the completed Website and for a monthly fee host it under the desired and available domain of the clients' choosing. Monthly fees range from $50 to $1500, depending on the website, the traffic, and minimal client revisions.


~ Clients who had Apps or Games made have the opportunity to either take the project as is, or for a percentage of the apps or games income, implement updates and improvments.

5. Copyright and Ownership

~ A final copy of our client's project will be given upon completion. However, clients won't fully own their project until it has been completly paid for. Clients who have fully paid for their project will get ownership of the final visible copy, not the ideas or thoughts that may have been brought up during development.


5.1 My Portfolio Content

~ Chaos & Art may want to use our client's project as an example of some of the work we do. Chaos & Art reserves the right to use extracts from the projects we've worked on for marketing and promotional purposes. However out of common courtesy, we will check in on a case to case basis with our clients informing on what we may use.

6. Contractor Information

~ Chaos & Art is a freelance contractor. We are not employees. As a freelance contractor we're free to work on our own schedule, set our own deadlines, set our own prices, work on projects from other clients, and we get to choose which projects we work on.


6.1 Illness or Injury

~ Things can change unexpectedly, and Chaos & Art may have to act accordingly with unplanned occurances. In the case this happens, we reserve the right to outsource our work to another contractor, postpone the job for a set period of time, or we may offer a full or partial refund.


~ If the project needs to be outsourced to another contractor, transitions and transfers of data will be done with the full permission and acknowledgement of our client, and with accordance to our Privacy Policy.
